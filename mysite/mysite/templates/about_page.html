About RoadScan AI

RoadScan AI is an end-to-end damage inspection platform powered by RF-DETR. Upload images or dashcam footage, and our system will automatically detect, classify, and visualize every crack, pothole, and surface deformation.

Under the Hood

  • Architecture: RF-DETR with a ResNet-18 Backbone
  • Data: RDD2020 split 70/15/15, converted from PascalVOC to COCO
  • Training: 150 epochs, mixed-precision, multi-scale augmentation, focal loss
  • Deployment: Flask + Django backend, serving via CV2 and Supervision annotations
